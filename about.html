CUSTOM TAILORING WITH A PERSONAL TOUCH

Clothing made for you — and only you.

Hi, I’m Rasa – the tailor and founder behind Le Ka.

This studio was born from a love of slow craft and quiet detail. I’ve always believed that clothing should serve the person, not the other way around. Not just fit in size, but in style, in purpose, in personality. The right piece can shift not only how someone looks — but how they carry themselves.

After years in fashion, design, and textile engineering, I chose made-to-measure because it allows for real connection. Every piece is one-of-one. Made by hand, shaped by conversation, and built to last.

This isn’t fast fashion. It’s something personal — something meaningful.

MY APPROACH

Every project starts the same way - not with a pattern, but with a conversation. We sit down. We talk. You share what makes you feel good, what you’re dressing for, what you’ve never quite found. I take your measurements, observe your posture, and help guide you through fabrics and finishes.

From there, I cut and create by hand. Whether it's a suit, coat, or shirt, the goal stays the same: a garment that feels like home on the body. Easy to move in. Easy to be in. Unmistakably yours.
Because when you feel seen in what you wear, something shifts - your posture, your confidence, your energy. That moment is why I do what I do.
